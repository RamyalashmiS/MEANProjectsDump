Bon Stay

Description:

Bon Stay is a hotel booking application wherein, you can search the available hotels and the details of the available rooms provided by each hotel. Using MEAN stack technology, this application is implemented.


User Stories :

US-01 - Signup
US-02 - Profile creation, Verification of User's Identity
US-03 - Login/Logout
US-04 - Display/Search Hotel details
US-05 - Book a room
US-06 - Add payment details
US-07 - Make payments
US-08 - Download Invoice
